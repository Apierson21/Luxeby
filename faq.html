Frequently Asked Questions


Orders

How long does shipping take?

Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days. Standard shipping usually takes 3–7 business days within the United States. Delivery times may vary during holidays or periods of high demand.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number so you can monitor your package's delivery status. You can also use the "Track Order" feature listed at the top of the screen.

Can I cancel or change my order?

If your order has not yet been processed, we'll do our best to accommodate changes or cancellations. Please contact our customer support team as soon as possible after placing your order.

Shipping

Do you offer free shipping?

We may offer free shipping during promotional periods or on qualifying orders. Any current shipping promotions will be displayed at checkout.

Do you ship internationally?

At this time, shipping availability depends on your location. Please enter your shipping address during checkout to see available delivery options.

My package says delivered, but I haven't received it. What should I do?

Please check around your property, with neighbors, or your building's mailroom. If you're still unable to locate your package, contact us within 7 days of the delivery date so we can assist you.

Products

Are your beauty tools suitable for all skin types?

Yes. Our beauty tools are designed to be gentle and suitable for most skin types. If you have a medical skin condition, we recommend consulting your healthcare provider before use.

How do I clean my beauty tools?

Wash your beauty tools with warm water and a gentle soap after use. Allow them to air dry completely before storing.

Can I use facial serum with my beauty tools?

Absolutely. For the best experience, we recommend applying your favorite facial serum or facial oil before using your jade roller or gua sha to help the tools glide smoothly across the skin.
