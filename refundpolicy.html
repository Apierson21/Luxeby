Refund Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

At LuxeBy, customer satisfaction is our priority. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

Return Eligibility

We accept returns within 30 days of the delivery date for eligible items. We reserve the right to refuse returns that do not meet these requirements.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Gift cards
  • Final Sale or clearence items
  • Products marked as non-returnable
  • Used or damaged products
  • Opened beauty products

How to Start a Return

To begin a return, contact our customer support team with your order number and the reason for your return. If your return is approved, we'll provide instructions for returning your item. Returns sent without prior authorization may not be accepted. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item was incorrect, damaged, or defective upon arrival.

Refunds

After we receive and inspect your returned item, we'll notify you of the status of your refund. If approved, your refund will be issued to your original payment method. Please allow 5–10 business days for the refund to appear on your account, depending on your financial institution. Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is the result of our error or a defective product.

Order Cancellations

Orders may be canceled or modified only if they have not entered the processing stage. Because we begin processing orders quickly, we cannot guarantee cancellation requests after an order has been placed.
